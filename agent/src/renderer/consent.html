Before you start

Desk Tracker is used by your company to log work hours.

While a timer is running, this app will:
  • Record the start and stop time of each session
  • Take a periodic screenshot of your screen (roughly every 3-5 minutes)
  • Measure your mouse and keyboard activity level — whether you were active, not what you typed or clicked on
  • Upload that data to your company's account for your manager to review
No screenshots or activity are recorded while the timer is stopped, and your keystrokes and click content are never captured — only activity presence.
